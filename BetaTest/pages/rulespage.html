BetaTest
Rules

Hunt will begin on 9th August at 00:00:00 AM and will end on 10th August at 11:59:59 PM.

BetaTest is a cryptic hunt, the participant has to make their way through multiple levels to reach the top of the leaderboard.

Not all levels are equal, some might be worth more points than others. The winner is decided by the points, not the level.

Each level will contain a question either in the form of text or a link or an image. Hints (text/link/image) may be provided as well to help the participant.

Besides, hints will be released on our official discord channel, the link to which is available on the official BetaTest website. It's highly recommended that you join it.

Answers will always be lower-case, alphanumeric and will contain no spaces. Special characters are not allowed. For example, if the answer is "Reg Exp /(.*)?/", you would type it in as “regexp”.

Every clue in the question is important. If it wasn't important, it wouldn't be there.

Beware of the spelling you enter, we do not autocorrect.

Do not spam admins or other participants, it may lead to your disqualification.

And if it was not obvious, team play, answer sharing, hint sharing and collaborating with other competitors in general is not allowed and any such evidence will lead to disqualification.
